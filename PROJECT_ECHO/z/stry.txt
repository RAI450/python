# # PROJECT ECHO

# ### *"Some experiments should never have been completed."*

# ---

# ## STORY

# **Year: 2048**

# Deep beneath the mountains, the **Helix Research Facility** was built in complete secrecy.

# Its mission was to develop a revolutionary energy source known only as **PROJECT ECHO**—an experiment that promised unlimited clean energy for the entire world.

# For years, the project remained hidden from the public.

# Then, one day...

# Everything went silent.

# All communication with the facility was suddenly lost.

# No distress signal was ever received.

# No scientist ever came out.

# The government immediately sealed the entire area, classified every document related to **PROJECT ECHO**, and declared the facility abandoned.

# No official explanation was ever released.

# No one ever discovered what truly happened inside.

# Among the missing scientists was one of the lead researchers...

# **Dr. Ethan Carter.**

# Your father.

# When you were a child, everyone told you he had died in the incident.

# But you never believed them.

# Deep in your heart, you always felt there was more to the story.

# For years, you searched for answers, but every official record had been erased.

# The truth seemed impossible to find.

# ---

# ## Fifteen Years Later...

# While cleaning the attic of your old family home, you discover a dusty wooden box hidden beneath the floorboards.

# The box is locked.

# After forcing it open, you find four objects inside.

# • A faded map of the Helix Research Facility.

# • An old **Level-1 Security Access Card** with your father's name printed on it.

# • A family photograph of you and your father standing in front of the Helix Research Facility on the day it opened.

# • A sealed envelope addressed to you.

# With trembling hands, you open the envelope.

# Inside is a handwritten note.

# ---

# **To my child,**

# **If you're reading this, then I was never able to come home.**

# **Everything you've been told about the Helix Research Facility is a lie.**

# **The truth was buried the day the facility was sealed.**

# **I cannot explain everything here. It isn't safe.**

# **If you truly want to know what happened...**

# **Follow the map.**

# **Trust your instincts.**

# **Trust no one.**

# **And whatever you do...**

# **Do not trust PROJECT ECHO.**

# **— Dad**

# ---

# You spend days studying the map.

# Every road...

# Every tunnel...

# Every hidden entrance...

# Eventually, your search leads you deep into the mountains.

# Standing before you is the forgotten entrance to the **Helix Research Facility**.

# The massive steel doors remain exactly as they were fifteen years ago.

# Silent.

# Dark.

# Waiting.

# Taking a deep breath, you swipe your father's old access card.

# A loud mechanical sound echoes through the mountain.

# **ACCESS GRANTED**

# The enormous doors slowly begin to open.

# Cold air rushes out from the darkness.

# You step inside.

# The doors slam shut behind you.

# **BOOM!**

# The lights immediately switch to flashing red.

# Sirens begin echoing through the empty corridors.

# A robotic voice breaks the silence.

# > **WARNING...**
# >
# > **LOCKDOWN PROTOCOL ACTIVATED.**
# >
# > **Power Failure Detected.**
# >
# > **Emergency Exit Locked.**
# >
# > **Facility Shutdown in Progress.**

# You rush back to the entrance.

# It won't open.

# You're trapped.

# A nearby monitor flickers to life.

# One sentence appears on the screen.

# > **"The truth lies ahead."**

# With no way back...

# You grip your flashlight, take a deep breath, and walk deeper into the abandoned facility.

# Somewhere inside these walls lies the truth about **PROJECT ECHO**...

# And the fate of your father.
